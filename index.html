My love,
Ading Maria

Today marks nine beautiful years since our journey began —
nine years of love, laughter, growth, challenges, and moments that shaped us.
And even after all this time… I still look at you and fall in love all over again.

In nine years, we’ve created memories that live deep in my bones —
the way your hand fits mine so naturally,
the way your voice calms the noise in my world,
the way your love makes me feel safe, seen, and deeply cherished.

But what amazes me most is that…
my love for you hasn’t stayed the same — it’s grown.
It has deepened with time,
softened with forgiveness,
and solidified through every ordinary moment we've chosen to stay.

Because the truth is…
we don’t always love each other the same every day.
Some days, love feels loud and easy.
Some days, love is quiet — hard, even.
There are days when we argue,
days when pride speaks louder than our hearts.
But no matter how heavy the day gets,
we always choose each other.
And somehow, after every fight,
we love each other even deeper.

You are my greatest comfort.
My calm in chaos.
My warmest home.
My favorite voice in a noisy world.
My one true love.

And after nine years, here’s what I know more than ever:
I choose you. Still. Always. Again and again.
I’ll keep choosing you — on the soft days,
on the stormy days,
on the in-between days.
And in every lifetime our souls are given the chance,
I’ll find you… and I’ll choose you.

Nine years behind us…
and yet it feels like we’re only just beginning.
There are still so many memories we’ve yet to create,
so many mornings I want to wake up beside you,
so many dreams I want to share with only you.

Thank you for loving me when I least deserved it.
Thank you for staying when it would’ve been easier to walk away.
Thank you for being my peace, my fire, and my forever.

Happy 9th Anniversary, my love.
Here’s to everything we’ve been,
everything we are,
and everything we’ll still become — together.

Ikaw ang pahinga…
at sa araw-araw na pag gising ko,
ikaw at ikaw pa rin ang pipiliin ko.

Forever yours,
Ading Jap
